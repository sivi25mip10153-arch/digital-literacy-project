Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
Always verify the sender before clicking on any link
Avoid downloading apps from unknown or untrusted sources
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Regularly check your bank account and transaction history
Do not trust calls claiming to be from banks asking for sensitive information
Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
Remember: UPI PIN is only required to SEND money, not to receive it
Always double-check the payment request details before approving Reporting Cybercrime:
