UPI / Online Payment Fraud – Case Study

Introduction: UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is widely used in India for fast and easy online payments. However, with its increasing use, cases of online payment fraud are also rising. Case Description: A college student received a call from a person pretending to be a bank official. The caller said that the student’s bank account would be blocked if it was not verified immediately. The fraudster then sent a UPI payment request and asked the student to enter the UPI PIN for verification. The student trusted the caller and entered the PIN. As a result, money was deducted from the student’s bank account. Analysis: The fraud happened because of a lack of awareness. The student did not know that: UPI PIN is used only to send money, not to receive it. Banks never ask for PIN, OTP, or personal details on calls. Consequences: The student lost money and faced stress. It was difficult to recover the amount because the payment was approved by the student. Conclusion: This case shows the importance of being careful while using digital payment systems. Awareness and caution can help prevent such frauds.
