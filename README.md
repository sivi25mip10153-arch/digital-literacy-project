## 📘 Project Overview

This Digital Literacy Portfolio has been developed as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project is designed to build essential digital skills required for academic, professional, and personal growth in today’s technology-driven world.

As a Student Digital Ambassador, the objective of this project is to help peers understand and effectively use digital tools, maintain a professional online presence, communicate responsibly, and stay safe in the digital environment.

The project consists of five structured tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy. All tasks are organized within this repository along with supporting files, screenshots, and a detailed project report.

---

## 🎯 Objectives of the Project

* To understand the concept and importance of digital literacy
* To develop a professional digital identity across multiple platforms
* To explore coding and collaboration tools used in academics and industry
* To learn and apply professional communication skills (email etiquette)
* To raise awareness about cybercrime and safe online practices

---

## 📂 Repository Structure

---

## 📝 Task Details

### 🔹 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

In this task, a visual infographic was created using an online design tool to explain the concept of digital literacy. It covers key topics such as safe internet usage, digital tools for students, and maintaining a professional online presence.

---

### 🔹 Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

Professional profiles were created/updated on platforms such as GitHub and LinkedIn. This task helped in understanding how to build an online presence that can be useful for internships, networking, and career opportunities.

---

### 🔹 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms

A beginner-level coding challenge was completed on a coding platform to develop problem-solving skills. Additionally, a Google Form was created to design a digital literacy awareness quiz, demonstrating the use of collaborative tools.

---

### 🔹 Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette

Two formal emails were drafted: one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying for an internship. A checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage was also created.

---

### 🔹 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

A case study on a selected cybercrime topic was developed, explaining how such crimes occur and their impact. A prevention checklist was also created to promote safe online behavior among students.
